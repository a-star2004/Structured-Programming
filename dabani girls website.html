About the School

Dabani Girls' School is an Educational Institution that provides quality Education to girls from various backgrounds.It is a leading and the only girls school in Busia,we are committed to empowering the girl child through quality education,discipline and spiritual growth.

School Offers and Facilities

Dabani Girls School offers both 'O' and A'level courses and has a range of facilities,including:

  • Classrooms:Well-equiped classrooms for effective learning
  • Laboratory:Science laboratories for practical lessons
  • Library:Resourceful libraries for research and study
  • Dormitories:Boarding facilities for students
  • Staff Quarters:Accomadation for staff members
  • High Speed internet:Access to internet research and learning.The school implements the new curiculum,focusing on ICT,teaching,and learning resources.
